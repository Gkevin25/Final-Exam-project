Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes available on our menu. Please check our menu for specific options or ask our staff for recommendations.

2. How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation by calling us at (+237) 6 77 58 88 67 or by using our online reservation system available on our website.

3. What are your delivery options?

We offer delivery through our website and popular delivery services. You can place your order online and choose your preferred delivery method.

4. Do you accommodate food allergies?

Yes, we take food allergies seriously. Please inform our staff of any allergies when placing your order, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

5. What are your payment options?

We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. Please check with our staff for more details.

6. Are you open on holidays?

Yes, we are open on most holidays. However, our hours may vary. Please check our website or call us for specific holiday hours.
